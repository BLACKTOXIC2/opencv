About OMR Scanner with AI

An advanced OMR scanning solution with AI-enhanced analysis

What is OMR Scanner?

OMR Scanner with AI is a powerful tool designed to process Optical Mark Recognition (OMR) sheets, such as multiple-choice answer sheets used in exams, surveys, and assessments. Our system combines advanced computer vision techniques with artificial intelligence to accurately detect and analyze marked bubbles on scanned answer sheets.

Unlike traditional OMR systems that require specialized scanners and pre-printed forms, our solution works with regular smartphone photos or scanned images of answer sheets. This makes it accessible to educators, trainers, and organizations of all sizes.

How It Works

  1. Upload a photo or scan of your OMR sheet
  2. Our system processes the image using computer vision algorithms
  3. Bubbles are detected and analyzed to determine which ones are marked
  4. Results are matched against an optional answer key (if provided)
  5. AI analysis verifies detection accuracy and provides insights
  6. Detailed results are presented with visual feedback

Technology Stack

  • Image Processing: OpenCV, NumPy
  • AI Integration: Google Gemini API
